How to Use AI Tools for Ad Ranking: Step-by-Step

Step 1: Set Up Conversion Tracking

Before using AI bidding strategies, implement proper conversion tracking in Google Ads or Facebook Ads Manager. Track purchases, form submissions, phone calls, or any valuable action. AI needs this data to optimize effectively.

Step 2: Enable Smart Bidding

Start with Target CPA or Maximize Conversions in Google Ads. Set realistic targets based on historical data. The AI will analyze thousands of signals (device, location, time, audience) to bid optimally for each auction.

Step 3: Generate High-Performing Creatives

Use AdCreative.ai to generate 50-100 ad variations. Upload your brand assets, select your campaign objective, and let AI create multiple versions. Test the top 5-10 predicted performers against your current best ads.

Step 4: Automate Optimization Rules

Set up automated rules in Revealbot or native platforms: pause ads with CTR below 1%, increase budget for ads with ROAS above 400%, notify when CPA exceeds target. This ensures continuous optimization without manual monitoring.
