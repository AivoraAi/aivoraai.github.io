🔍 What Is Reisen mit Meilen Kurs?

Reisen mit Meilen Kurs is a digital training program designed to teach travelers how to leverage airline loyalty programs, credit card rewards, and travel hacking strategies to reduce travel costs — often by 50–90%.

Core modules typically include:

  • ✅ Understanding airline alliances (Star Alliance, Oneworld, SkyTeam)
  • ✅ Best credit cards for earning miles in German-speaking countries
  • ✅ Transferable points systems (Amex Membership Rewards, Chase Ultimate Rewards equivalents)
  • ✅ Finding award seat availability and booking strategies
  • ✅ Maximizing hotel points and elite status benefits
  • ✅ Avoiding common mistakes that devalue your miles

❌ Not Ideal For: Travelers exclusively using non-European airlines · Those seeking advanced award chart arbitrage strategies · People outside Europe needing region-specific card recommendations

❌ Cons of Reisen mit Meilen Kurs

  • ⚠️ Regional Focus: Strategies optimized for DACH region may not directly apply to travelers in India, Southeast Asia, Africa, or the Americas without adaptation.
  • 🔄 Program Changes: Airline loyalty programs change frequently. Course content requires periodic updates to stay current.
  • 💳 Credit Requirements: Many high-value cards require good credit history — a barrier for some beginners or those in countries with different credit systems.
  • 🌐 Language Barrier for Non-German Speakers: If you don't speak German, the course won't be accessible (though this is by design).
  • 💰 Upsells: Like many digital courses, expect optional upgrades for advanced modules or coaching.

🌍 Can You Use This Course Outside German-Speaking Countries?

Yes — but with adaptation. Here's how travelers worldwide can benefit:

  • 🇮🇳 India / 🇵🇭 Philippines / 🌍 Africa: Core concepts (alliances, transfer partners, award search) are universal. Use the framework, then research local credit cards and airline partners. Focus on programs with global reach: Star Alliance (Lufthansa, Singapore Airlines), Oneworld (British Airways, Qatar), SkyTeam (Air France, Delta).
  • 🇺🇸 USA / 🇬🇧 UK / 🇦🇺 Australia: Many strategies transfer directly. Supplement with region-specific resources (e.g., The Points Guy for US cards). The course's award booking methodology is globally applicable.
  • 🌎 Latin America: Focus on alliances with strong LATAM presence: SkyTeam (Aeroméxico, Air Europa), Oneworld (LATAM Airlines). Use the course's search techniques, then adapt card recommendations to your country.

🔑 Key Insight: Travel hacking principles are universal. The course teaches the how; you apply it to your region's what (cards, airlines, programs).

✅ How to Get Maximum Value from Reisen mit Meilen Kurs

Follow this workflow to turn knowledge into savings:

  1. Complete Core Modules First: Don't jump to booking. Master the fundamentals: how alliances work, how points transfer, how to search for awards.
  2. Map to Your Region: After learning a concept, research its local equivalent. Example: Course teaches Amex MR → Lufthansa. You research: "What transferable points do I have? Which airlines serve my region?"
  3. Start Small: Book one simple award flight first (e.g., domestic or short-haul). Build confidence before attempting complex multi-city itineraries.
  4. Join the Community: Ask questions, share wins, learn from others' mistakes. The private group is often where the real value lives.
  5. Track Your Savings: Document every award booking: cash price vs. points cost. Seeing €500+ savings on one flight motivates continued learning.
  6. Reinvest Savings: Use money saved on flights to fund more travel — or invest in premium experiences (business class, luxury hotels) you couldn't otherwise afford.
