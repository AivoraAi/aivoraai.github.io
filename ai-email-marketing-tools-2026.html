How to choose the right AI email tool

Consider these factors when selecting a platform:

✅ Key Factors:

  • List Size: Free tiers often limit contacts; paid plans scale with volume
  • Features Needed: Do you need just sending, or also CRM, automation, and landing pages?
  • Integration: Does it connect with your existing stack (Shopify, WordPress, etc.)?
  • Deliverability: Check sender reputation and inbox placement rates
  • Support: Look for responsive customer service and resources
  • Budget: Balance features with cost ($0-$500+/month)

⭐ Pro Tip: Start with an all-in-one platform like Mailchimp or HubSpot if you're new. Add specialized tools (like Jasper or Phrasee) as you scale and need advanced features.

Building your AI email workflow

Combine these tools into an efficient system:

Phase 1: Strategy & Setup (Week 1)

  • Choose your main ESP (Email Service Provider)
  • Import and clean your list
  • Set up basic segments
  • Design branded templates

Phase 2: Content Creation (Ongoing)

  • Use Jasper or Copy.ai for draft copy
  • Generate subject lines with Phrasee or ESP tools
  • Personalize content dynamically
  • Test designs in Litmus

Phase 3: Automation & Optimization (Month 2+)

  • Set up welcome series and abandoned cart flows
  • Enable send time optimization
  • A/B test subject lines and content
  • Analyze performance and refine

✅ Expected Results

With this workflow, businesses typically see:
• Month 1: 10-20% higher open rates
• Month 2: 15-30% increase in CTR
• Month 3: 20-50% revenue growth from email
• Month 6: Significant time savings (10-20 hours/week)

Best practices for AI email marketing

Maximize results while maintaining trust:

1. Keep It Human

  • Review all AI-generated copy for tone and accuracy
  • Add personal anecdotes and brand voice
  • Avoid overly robotic language

2. Respect Privacy

  • Be transparent about data usage
  • Provide easy unsubscribe options
  • Comply with GDPR/CCPA regulations

3. Test Continuously

  • A/B test subject lines, send times, and content
  • Monitor deliverability metrics
  • Adjust strategy based on data

4. Segment Smartly

  • Use AI to identify behavioral patterns
  • Create dynamic segments
  • Send relevant content to each group

💡 Remember: AI is a powerful assistant, but your strategy, creativity, and understanding of your audience still matter most. Use AI to handle the heavy lifting so you can focus on building relationships and driving value.

Final tips for email success

Follow these best practices:

  • Clean Your List: Remove inactive subscribers regularly
  • Mobile First: Ensure emails look great on phones
  • Clear CTAs: One primary call-to-action per email
  • Value First: Provide useful content, not just promotions
  • Consistent Schedule: Stick to a regular sending frequency
  • Monitor Metrics: Track opens, clicks, conversions, and unsubscribes
