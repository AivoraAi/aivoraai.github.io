About Varun

I started my digital marketing journey in 2019, and quickly became fascinated by how AI tools were changing the way content creators, freelancers, and entrepreneurs work. In 2023, I founded Aivora AI with a simple mission: give honest, independent reviews of AI tools so creators can make confident decisions without wasting money on the wrong tools.

Unlike review sites that accept payments for rankings, I personally test every tool I review. I sign up, use it for real tasks — writing, video editing, social media, real estate marketing, SEO — and report exactly what I find. No paid placements. No inflated scores.

My Review Process

1. Sign Up Personally

I sign up for the free trial or purchase a plan with my own money. No free review copies that bias opinions.

2. Real-World Testing

Test the tool on actual tasks for a minimum of 7-30 days. Writing articles, editing videos, generating images — real work.

3. Compare Competitors

Test against 3-5 competing tools in the same category to understand relative strengths and weaknesses.

4. Document Everything

Record time saved, quality of output, limitations, pricing value, and ease of use. No detail is too small.

5. Update Regularly

Reviews are updated when tools change features, pricing, or AI models. Old reviews are re-tested quarterly.

How I Approach AI Visibility & SEO

🎯 AI Visibility (GEO)

I don't chase fake hacks. I build content that AI engines can actually understand, trust, and cite. This means:

  • Clear definitions at the start of every section
  • Structured data (JSON-LD) on every page
  • Real statistics and citations from trusted sources
  • Content updated quarterly to stay current
  • Comparison tables AI can easily extract
  • FAQ sections that match real user questions

Results take 3-6 months — not overnight. I track manually across ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity, and Copilot to see if Aivora AI gets mentioned.

Based on Princeton GEO research: targeted optimization can boost AI citation rates by up to 40%.

🔍 Traditional SEO

SEO is the foundation. Without it, GEO doesn't work. My approach is straightforward:

  • Keyword research using real search data
  • Internal linking between related articles
  • Fast page speed and mobile optimization
  • Quality content that answers real questions
  • Backlinks from relevant, trusted sites
  • Regular content audits and updates

I don't use black-hat techniques, AI-generated spam, or keyword stuffing. Every article is written to help readers first, rank second.

SEO gets you on search pages. GEO gets you inside AI answers. Both matter.
