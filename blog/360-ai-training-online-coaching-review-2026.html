What Is 360 AI Training?

360 AI Training isn't just a collection of video tutorials. It’s a structured 8-week hybrid coaching program that combines recorded lessons with live Q&A sessions. The goal is simple: to turn AI curiosity into a profitable skill set.

The curriculum covers everything from basic prompt engineering to advanced automation workflows using tools like Make and Zapier. It’s designed for students, professionals, and entrepreneurs who want to future-proof their careers.

Deep Dive: What’s Inside?

The course is divided into 8 core modules. Here is what we found most valuable:

🚀 Module 1-2: The Foundation

Most courses skip this, but 360 AI starts with the "AI Mindset." You learn how to think like an operator, not just a user. The prompt engineering section is deep, covering chain-of-thought prompting and role-playing techniques that actually work.

📝 Module 3-4: Content & Automation

This is where the magic happens. You learn to create blogs, YouTube scripts, and social posts in minutes. But the real value is in Module 4, where they teach you to automate these workflows. If you want to build an instant AI agency, this module is your blueprint.

💼 Module 5-8: Monetization

They don't just teach you skills; they teach you how to sell them. From Upwork proposals to creating digital products on Gumroad, this section is pure gold for anyone looking to generate AI revenue formulas that print money.

Is 360 AI Training suitable for complete beginners?

Yes, absolutely. The course starts from scratch, assuming no prior technical knowledge. It guides you step-by-step through the basics before moving to advanced topics.

Do I get a certificate upon completion?

Yes, you receive a digital certificate of completion that you can add to your LinkedIn profile or resume to showcase your new skills.

How is this different from free YouTube tutorials?

YouTube gives you information; 360 AI gives you a system. The structured curriculum, live mentorship, and community support save you hundreds of hours of trial and error.
