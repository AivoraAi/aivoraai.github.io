🧠 What Exactly is AI Beginner Course?

AI Beginner Course is a comprehensive, video-based training program that breaks down the vast, intimidating world of artificial intelligence into simple, everyday language. The core philosophy of the course is "application over theory." You won't be learning the mathematical history of the perceptron; you will be learning how to get AI to write a professional email, summarize a 50-page PDF, or generate an image for your business.

The course is structured to remove friction. Every video is under 10 minutes. Every tool introduced is either free or has a generous free tier. Every concept is explained using real-world analogies that a 12-year-old could understand.

Core Curriculum Includes:

  • 40+ bite-sized video lessons (5-10 minutes each)
  • Downloadable cheat sheets, prompt libraries, and workflow templates
  • Private community access (active Facebook/Discord groups)
  • Weekly live Q&A sessions with instructors
  • Lifetime updates (crucial in AI, where tools change monthly)
