Understanding YouTube Shorts Script Format

Blog posts and YouTube Shorts scripts are fundamentally different. Here's what you need to know:

⏱️ Length

60 Seconds Maximum

150-180 words max. Every second counts. No fluff, no long introductions, get to the point immediately.

🎯 Hook

3-Second Rule

You have 3 seconds to grab attention. Start with a question, shocking stat, or bold statement.

💬 Tone

Conversational

Write like you're talking to a friend. Short sentences. Contractions. Natural language.

Visual Cues

Show, Don't Tell

Include notes for visuals, text overlays, and transitions. Video is visual first.

The Perfect YouTube Shorts Script Structure

🎬 The 60-Second Formula

0-3 seconds: HOOK (Grab attention immediately)
3-10 seconds: PROBLEM/QUESTION (Create curiosity)
10-50 seconds: SOLUTION/VALUE (Deliver 3 key points)
50-60 seconds: CALL-TO-ACTION (Tell them what to do next)

Example:
"Stop wasting hours writing blog posts. [HOOK]
Here's how I write 10 posts in 1 hour using AI. [PROBLEM]
Step 1: Use ChatGPT to outline. Step 2: Expand with Claude. Step 3: Edit with Grammarly. [SOLUTION]
Follow for more AI writing hacks. [CTA]"

Method 1: Manual AI Conversion (Best Quality)

This method gives you the highest quality scripts. It takes 10-15 minutes per Short but produces viral-worthy content.

Extract Key Points from Blog Post

Don't feed the entire blog post to AI. First, identify the 3-5 most valuable points. These will become your script's core content.

Example extraction:
Blog post: "10 AI Tools for Content Creators" (2,500 words)
Key points for Short:
• ChatGPT for writing
• Midjourney for images
• Descript for video editing

Use This Exact AI Prompt

Copy this prompt and customize it with your key points:

# YouTube Shorts Script Generator Prompt Convert the following blog post key points into a 60-second YouTube Shorts script: KEY POINTS: [Paste your 3-5 key points here] REQUIREMENTS: - Total length: 150-180 words maximum - Structure: Hook (3s) → Problem (7s) → Solution (40s) → CTA (10s) - Tone: Conversational, energetic, engaging - Language: Simple, short sentences - Include: Visual cues in [brackets] - End with: Clear call-to-action FORMAT: [0-3s] HOOK: Shocking stat or question [3-10s] PROBLEM: Why this matters [10-50s] SOLUTION: 3 key points with visual cues [50-60s] CTA: What to do next TOPIC: [Your blog post topic]

Refine and Add Personality

AI gives you a solid foundation. Now make it yours:

  • Add your unique perspective or experience
  • Include specific examples from your life
  • Adjust pacing for natural speech
  • Read it aloud and time it
  • Cut anything that feels forced

✅ Pro Tip: Record yourself reading the script before filming. If you stumble over words or run out of breath, rewrite those sections. Natural delivery beats perfect words every time.

Advanced AI Prompts for Different Niches

Different content types need different approaches. Here are specialized prompts:

Educational/Tutorial Content

Create a YouTube Shorts script teaching [TOPIC] in 60 seconds: Structure: - Hook: "Most people do [common mistake]" - Problem: Why it matters - Solution: 3-step process - CTA: "Save this for later" Tone: Teacher-like, encouraging Include: Step numbers on screen

List-Based Content

Convert this list into a fast-paced YouTube Shorts script: Format: - Hook: "Here are [X] [things] you need" - Rapid-fire list: 1 second per item - Best item: Spend 10 seconds here - CTA: "Which is your favorite?" Pacing: Quick, energetic Visuals: Text overlay for each item

Story/Case Study Content

Transform this case study into a storytelling Shorts script: Structure: - Hook: "I went from [before] to [after]" - Struggle: The problem I faced - Solution: What I did differently - Result: Specific numbers/outcome - CTA: "Comment [keyword] for details" Tone: Personal, vulnerable, inspiring

Ask Questions

  • "I tried [thing] for 30 days" — Personal experiment hooks
  • "[Number] things I wish I knew" — List-based curiosity

Common Mistakes to Avoid

✅ Do This

  • Keep scripts under 180 words
  • Read aloud before filming
  • Add text overlays for key points
  • Use trending audio when relevant
  • Include clear CTA in every Short
  • Post consistently (3-5x per week)

❌ Don't Do This

  • Don't copy blog post word-for-word
  • Don't skip the hook—always start strong
  • Don't ignore audio quality
  • Don't forget to add captions
  • Don't use jargon or complex words
  • Don't post without thumbnail optimization
