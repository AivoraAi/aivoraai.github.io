How to Build an AI Landing Page in 10 Minutes

Ready to build? Here is the exact step-by-step process we use to launch pages quickly:

Step 1: Choose Your Builder

Use Framer AI if you want a beautiful, highly customized portfolio or startup site. Use Durable if you need a local service business site up instantly with zero hassle.

Step 2: Write a Detailed Prompt

The better your prompt, the better your site. Don't just say "Coffee shop." Say: "A modern, minimalist coffee shop in Seattle with a dark theme, an online menu, and a reservation form." Specificity is key.

Step 3: Customize with AI

Once the AI generates the initial layout, use its built-in tools to rewrite the copy, generate new images, or adjust the color palette to match your exact brand guidelines.

Step 4: Optimize for SEO & Publish

Ensure your meta titles and descriptions are auto-generated correctly. Check mobile responsiveness on your phone, connect your custom domain, and hit publish!
