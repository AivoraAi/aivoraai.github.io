How to Build an AI Landing Page in 10 Minutes

Step 1: Choose Your Builder

Use Framer AI if you want a beautiful portfolio or startup site. Use Durable if you need a service business site up instantly.

Step 2: Write a Detailed Prompt

The better your prompt, the better your site. Don't just say "Coffee shop." Say "A modern, minimalist coffee shop in Seattle with a dark theme, an online menu, and a reservation form."

Step 3: Customize with AI

Once the AI generates the layout, use its built-in AI tools to rewrite the copy, generate new images, or adjust the color palette to match your brand.

Step 4: Optimize for SEO

Ensure your meta titles and descriptions are auto-generated correctly. Check mobile responsiveness on all devices before hitting publish.
