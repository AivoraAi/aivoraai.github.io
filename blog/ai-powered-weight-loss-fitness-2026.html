Phase 01

🔍 Deep Assessment & Baseline

During Week 1, the AI ingests your data: age, weight, body fat percentage, blood work (if available), dietary preferences, allergies, and daily schedule. It calculates your exact basal metabolic rate (BMR) and establishes your baseline.

Phase 02

🍽️ Algorithmic Plan Creation

The AI generates a custom meal plan and workout routine. It doesn't just give you "chicken and rice." It scans local grocery databases, suggests recipes based on your flavor profile, and generates automated grocery lists optimized for your budget.

Phase 03

📊 Implementation & Biofeedback

As you execute the plan, you log daily metrics: weight, sleep quality, stress levels, and workout completion. The AI compares your actual biofeedback against its predicted trajectory.

Phase 04

🔄 Micro-Adjustments

This is where the magic happens. If your weight loss stalls, the AI doesn't just say "eat less." It analyzes your sleep data, realizes your cortisol is high, and adjusts your plan to include more recovery and anti-inflammatory foods.
