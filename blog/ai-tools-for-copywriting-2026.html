How to Use AI Tools for Copywriting: Step-by-Step

Step 1: Define Your Copy Objective

Identify what you're writing: Facebook ad, sales page, email sequence, or product description. Different copy types require different structures, tones, and calls-to-action. Choose the AI tool that specializes in your specific copy type.

Step 2: Set Up Brand Voice

Train your AI tool with examples of your brand's voice, tone, and style. Most platforms like Jasper AI and Copy.ai allow you to input brand guidelines so the AI generates copy that matches your unique voice consistently.

Step 3: Generate & Refine

Use the appropriate template or prompt to generate your first draft. For ads, use PAS (Problem-Agitate-Solution) framework. For sales pages, use AIDA (Attention-Interest-Desire-Action). Review and refine the AI output for emotional resonance and conversion optimization.

Step 4: Test & Optimize

Run A/B tests on AI-generated copy vs. human-written copy. Track conversion rates, click-through rates, and engagement metrics. Use the data to refine your AI prompts and improve future outputs.
