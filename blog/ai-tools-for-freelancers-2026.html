How to Build Your AI Freelance Stack

Setting up your AI workflow can feel overwhelming, but if you break it down into these steps, you'll be scaling your business in no time.

Step 1
🔍 Audit Your Workflow

Track your time for one week. Identify repetitive tasks like admin, invoicing, or basic content drafting that consume your day.

Step 2
🛠️ Choose First Tool

Pick one high-impact area. If you're in e-commerce, look into AI automation tools for ecommerce to see how much you can scale.

Step 3
⚡ Implement & Train

Set up the tool, integrate with your existing apps, and establish guidelines for AI-generated content to maintain quality.

Step 4
📈 Measure & Scale

Track time saved and client satisfaction. Once profitable, add more AI tools, take on more clients, and raise your rates.
