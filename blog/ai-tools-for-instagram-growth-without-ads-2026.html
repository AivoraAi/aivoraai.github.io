Organic Growth Mistakes to Avoid

Even with the best AI tools, you can sabotage your growth if you fall into these traps:

❌ Mistakes to Avoid

  • Buying Followers: Kills engagement and triggers shadowbans instantly.
  • Using Generic Hashtags: #love or #instagood will bury your post. Use Flick for niche tags.
  • Posting Randomly: Inconsistent timing confuses the algorithm. Schedule with Later.
  • Ignoring DMs: The algorithm rewards accounts that foster conversations. Use ManyChat.
  • Raw AI Output: Never post unedited AI captions. Add your human personality and humor.

✅ Best Practices

  • Focus on Reels: Short-form video is still the #1 way to reach non-followers.
  • Engage 15m/Day: Spend time replying to comments and engaging with niche peers.
  • Use ManyChat Keywords: Tell users to "Comment WORD" to boost comment metrics.
  • Batch Create: Use AI to design a month of content in one weekend.
  • Analyze Weekly: Double down on formats that get the most Saves and Shares.
