How to Use AI Tools for Course Creation: Step-by-Step

Step 1: Generate Course Outline with Teachable AI

Use Teachable's AI course outline generator: "Create a comprehensive course outline for [topic] targeting [audience]. Include 8 modules with 5 lessons each, learning objectives, and practical exercises."

Review and customize the AI-generated outline. This saves 2-3 hours of planning time.

Step 2: Create Videos with Synthesia

Write your script or use ChatGPT to generate it. Upload to Synthesia, choose an AI avatar, select voice and language. The AI generates professional presenter videos in minutes — no camera, lighting, or actors needed.

Step 3: Edit & Polish with Pictory/Descript

Use Pictory to create course trailers and summaries from your full-length videos. Use Descript for transcription, removing filler words, and adding captions. Both tools save 5-10 hours per course module.
