How to Use AI Tools for Selling Digital Products: Step-by-Step

Step 1: Choose Your Product Type

Decide between templates (Canva, Notion), printables (planners, worksheets), ebooks/guides (ChatGPT), digital art (Leonardo AI), or courses. Each has different creation and sales requirements.

Step 2: Create Products with AI

Use ChatGPT to generate content: "Create a 30-page ebook about [topic] with chapters, examples, and actionable tips." Use Canva AI to design: "Create a printable planner template with weekly and monthly pages."

Step 3: Set Up Sales Platform

Create a Gumroad account, upload your digital product, set pricing, and write a compelling description (use ChatGPT: "Write a persuasive product description for [product] highlighting benefits"). Add product images created in Canva.

Step 4: Automate & Market

Use Zapier to automate: when someone buys, add them to your email list, send a thank you message, and notify you on Slack. Use Canva AI to create social media posts promoting your product. Schedule posts in advance.
