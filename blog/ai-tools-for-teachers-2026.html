How to Use AI Tools for Teaching: Step-by-Step

Step 1: Plan Lessons with ChatGPT

Use this prompt: "Create a 45-minute lesson plan for [grade level] on [topic]. Include: learning objectives aligned to [standard], engaging hook, direct instruction, guided practice, independent practice, and assessment. Differentiate for struggling and advanced learners."

Review the output, adjust for your students' needs, and add your personal teaching style. This saves 30-45 minutes per lesson.

Step 2: Create Visuals with Canva AI

Go to Canva, search "worksheet" or "presentation," select a template, and use Magic Design to auto-generate content. Use AI image generation for custom illustrations. Resize once for all formats (handout, slide, poster) using Magic Resize.

Step 3: Differentiate with Diffit

Paste any text, article, or topic into Diffit. The AI generates multiple reading levels, summaries, vocabulary lists, and comprehension questions automatically—saving 20-30 minutes per differentiation task.
