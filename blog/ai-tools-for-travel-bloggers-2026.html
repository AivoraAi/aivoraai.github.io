How Travel Bloggers Use AI: Step-by-Step

Step 1: Plan with Roam Around

Before you book, enter your destination and dates into Roam Around. Get a full day-by-day plan. Copy this itinerary into a document.

Step 2: Create Content Briefs with ChatGPT

Paste your itinerary into ChatGPT and ask: "Create a list of 10 blog post titles and Instagram captions for this trip." This gives you a content calendar before you even leave home.

Step 3: Film & Clip with OpusClip

Film your experiences freely. At night or on rest days, upload your footage to OpusClip. It will find the best moments and turn them into Reels/TikToks while you sleep.

Step 4: Write & Publish

Use ChatGPT to draft your blog post based on your notes. Add your personal photos and unique insights (the "human touch") and hit publish.
