How to Use AI Tools for Video Shorts: Step-by-Step

Step 1: Repurpose Long Videos with OpusClip

Upload your podcast, webinar, or YouTube video to OpusClip. The AI automatically identifies viral moments, creates multiple shorts, adds captions, and optimizes for each platform. Review and export in 5 minutes.

Step 2: Edit & Polish with Descript

Import your short into Descript. Use the text transcript to remove filler words, awkward pauses, and mistakes. Apply Studio Sound to clean up audio. Add B-roll or screen recordings if needed. Export in vertical format.

Step 3: Create from Scratch with InVideo AI

For faceless content, use InVideo AI. Type your topic: "Create a 60-second short about AI tools for content creators." The AI writes the script, finds stock footage, adds voiceover, and edits everything together. Customize as needed and export.

Step 4: Optimize for Each Platform

Use platform-specific optimizations: TikTok (trending sounds, text overlays), Instagram Reels (trending audio, hashtags), YouTube Shorts (SEO titles, end screens). Most AI tools auto-optimize, but manual tweaks improve performance.
