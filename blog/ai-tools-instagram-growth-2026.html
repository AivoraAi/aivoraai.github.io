Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Will using AI get my account banned?

A: No! Using AI for content creation, scheduling, and analytics is completely safe. Instagram encourages automation tools. Just avoid spammy behavior like mass following/unfollowing.

Q: Do I need technical skills?

A: Not at all! Modern AI tools are designed for non-technical users. If you can use Instagram, you can use these tools.

Q: How long until I see results?

A: Most users see improved engagement within 2-4 weeks. Follower growth typically accelerates after 1-2 months of consistent AI-optimized posting.

Q: Can AI replace human creativity?

A: No, AI enhances it. Use AI for efficiency and data, but add your unique personality and perspective. The best results come from AI + human touch.

Q: Which tool should I start with?

A: Start with a scheduling tool like Later or Buffer (both have free tiers), plus an AI caption generator. Master these before adding more tools.
