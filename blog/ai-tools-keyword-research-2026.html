How to Use AI Tools for Keyword Research: Step-by-Step

Step 1: Start with Seed Keywords

Enter your main topic or product into Ahrefs or SEMrush. For example, if you sell "organic coffee," start with that term. The AI will generate hundreds of related keywords with search volumes, difficulty scores, and traffic potential.

Step 2: Analyze Competitor Keywords

Use the competitor analysis feature to see what keywords your top competitors rank for. Enter their domain into Ahrefs Site Explorer or SEMrush Domain Analytics. Look for keywords they rank for that you don't—these are your content gaps and opportunities.

Step 3: Find Long-Tail Opportunities

Use AnswerThePublic or the keyword suggestions in Ahrefs/SEMrush to find long-tail variations. These longer, more specific keywords have lower competition and higher conversion rates. Look for question-based keywords and specific user intents.

Step 4: Optimize Content with Surfer SEO

Once you've selected your target keywords, use Surfer SEO to analyze the top 10 ranking pages. It will tell you exactly which keywords to include, optimal word count, and content structure. Follow the recommendations to create content that's optimized to rank.
