How to Use AI Tools for Real Estate Marketing: Step-by-Step

Step 1: Write Listing Descriptions with ChatGPT

Open ChatGPT and use this prompt: "Write a compelling real estate listing description for a 3-bedroom, 2-bathroom home in [location]. Features include [list features]. Target buyer: [buyer type]. Tone: warm and professional. Length: 150 words."

Review the output, adjust for accuracy, and add your personal touch. This alone saves 30–45 minutes per listing.

Step 2: Create Social Media Posts with Canva AI

Go to Canva, search "real estate" in templates, select a property post template, and use Magic Replace to swap in your property photos. Use the AI caption generator for Instagram or Facebook copy. Resize once for all platforms using Magic Resize.

Step 3: Schedule Everything with Buffer

Connect your Facebook, Instagram, and LinkedIn to Buffer. Schedule your property posts, open house announcements, and market updates in batches. Buffer's AI assistant can suggest optimal posting times based on your audience's engagement patterns.
