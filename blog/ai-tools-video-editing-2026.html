Top AI Tools for Video Editing in 2026

1. AI Automated Cutting & Trimming

✂️ Descript

Descript is the gold standard for AI video editing. It transcribes your video, and you edit the video by editing the text. Delete a word in the transcript, and it cuts that part of the video automatically.

  • Best For: Podcasters, YouTubers, and talking-head videos
  • Key Features: Text-based editing, filler word removal (um, uh), studio sound enhancement, eye contact correction
  • Price: Free tier, $24-33/month
  • Pro Tip: Use the "Remove Filler Words" feature to instantly clean up your talking-head videos without manual cutting.

✂️ Wisecut

Wisecut automatically cuts dead space, adds background music, and creates subtitles. Perfect for creators who want to edit videos without learning complex software.

  • Best For: Beginners, vloggers, and quick social media videos
  • Key Features: Auto-cut silence, auto-ducking (lowers music when you speak), auto-subtitles
  • Price: Free tier, $15-25/month

2. AI Background Removal & Green Screen

🎨 RunwayML

RunwayML is the most powerful AI video editing suite. It includes "Green Screen" (AI background removal without a green screen), "Inpainting" (remove objects from video), and "Gen-2" (text-to-video generation).

  • Best For: Professional editors, filmmakers, and VFX artists
  • Key Features: Green Screen (no green screen needed), Inpainting, Gen-2 text-to-video, motion tracking
  • Price: Free tier, $12-76/month
  • Pro Tip: Use "Green Screen" to remove backgrounds from talking-head videos without needing a physical green screen.

🎨 Unscreen

Unscreen automatically removes backgrounds from videos and GIFs. Perfect for creating transparent video overlays and memes.

  • Best For: Quick background removal, meme creation, video overlays
  • Key Features: Auto background removal, transparent video export, GIF support
  • Price: Free tier, $9-49/month

3. AI B-Roll & Stock Footage Generation

🎬 Pictory

Pictory turns blog posts, scripts, and text into videos with AI-generated B-roll. Perfect for repurposing content and creating faceless videos.

  • Best For: Repurposing blog posts, creating faceless videos, video summaries
  • Key Features: Text-to-video, auto B-roll selection, auto-subtitles, video resizing
  • Price: $19-99/month
  • Pro Tip: Paste your blog post URL into Pictory to automatically generate a video summary with relevant B-roll.

🎬 InVideo AI

InVideo AI generates complete videos from text prompts. You describe the video, and it generates the script, selects stock footage, adds voiceover, and edits everything together.

  • Best For: Faceless YouTube channels, marketing videos, explainer videos
  • Key Features: Text-to-video, AI scriptwriting, auto B-roll, AI voiceover, auto-subtitles
  • Price: Free tier, $20-60/month

4. AI Audio Enhancement & Captioning

🎙️ Adobe Podcast (Enhance Speech)

Adobe's Enhance Speech uses AI to make your audio sound like it was recorded in a professional studio. It removes background noise, echo, and enhances voice clarity.

  • Best For: Podcasters, YouTubers, and anyone with poor audio quality
  • Key Features: Noise removal, echo reduction, voice enhancement, studio sound quality
  • Price: Free (currently in beta)
  • Pro Tip: Run your podcast audio through Enhance Speech before uploading to make it sound like it was recorded in a $10,000 studio.

🎙️ Opus Clip

Opus Clip automatically repurposes long-form videos into viral short-form clips. It identifies the most engaging moments, adds captions, and resizes for TikTok, Reels, and Shorts.

  • Best For: Repurposing long videos into shorts, TikTok creators, podcasters
  • Key Features: Auto-clip generation, viral moment detection, auto-captions, auto-resize
  • Price: Free tier, $15-49/month
  • Pro Tip: Upload your 1-hour podcast and let Opus Clip generate 10 viral TikTok clips automatically.

⭐ Pro Tip: The "AI Video Editing Stack" that top creators use: Descript (editing) + RunwayML (background removal) + Opus Clip (repurposing) + Adobe Podcast (audio enhancement). This stack can cut your editing time by 80% while improving quality.

Step-by-Step AI Video Editing Workflow

Step 1: Record & Import (5 mins)

  • Record your video (talking head, vlog, podcast)
  • Import into Descript for text-based editing
  • Let Descript transcribe your video automatically

Step 2: Edit by Editing Text (15 mins)

  • Edit the transcript like a document
  • Delete filler words (um, uh, like) with one click
  • Cut dead space by deleting text
  • Use "Studio Sound" to enhance audio quality

Step 3: Remove Backgrounds & Add B-Roll (10 mins)

  • Export video and import into RunwayML
  • Use "Green Screen" to remove backgrounds without a green screen
  • Add B-roll from Pictory or stock footage libraries

Step 4: Repurpose into Shorts (5 mins)

  • Upload final video to Opus Clip
  • Let AI identify viral moments and generate clips
  • Export clips for TikTok, Reels, and Shorts

Best Practices & Pitfalls to Avoid

1. Don't Over-Edit with AI

AI is great for cutting dead space and removing backgrounds, but don't let it remove personality. Keep natural pauses and authentic moments that make your content relatable.

2. Always Review AI-Generated Content

AI B-roll generators can sometimes select irrelevant footage. Always review and swap out clips that don't match your message.

3. Enhance Audio First

Poor audio quality kills retention. Always run your audio through Adobe Podcast Enhance Speech before editing. Good audio is more important than good video.

4. Repurpose Everything

Every long-form video should be repurposed into 5-10 short-form clips. Use Opus Clip to automatically generate TikTok, Reels, and Shorts clips from your long videos.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can AI really edit videos as well as a human editor?

A: AI is excellent for repetitive tasks like cutting dead space, removing backgrounds, and generating captions. However, creative decisions like storytelling, pacing, and emotional impact still require human judgment. Use AI for the heavy lifting, then add your creative touch.

Q: Do I need expensive software to use AI video editing tools?

A: No! Most AI video editing tools are cloud-based and work in your browser. Descript, RunwayML, and Opus Clip all have free tiers that are sufficient for beginners. As you scale, you can upgrade to paid plans.

Q: Will AI replace video editors?

A: AI won't replace video editors, but video editors who use AI will replace those who don't. AI handles the tedious tasks, freeing you up to focus on creative decisions and storytelling.

Q: How do I choose the right AI video editing tool?

A: Start with your biggest bottleneck. If editing takes too long, use Descript. If you need background removal, use RunwayML. If you need to repurpose long videos into shorts, use Opus Clip. Build your stack based on your specific needs.
