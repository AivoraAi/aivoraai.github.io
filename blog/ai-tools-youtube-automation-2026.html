✂️ Descript ⭐ Best for Editing
★★★★★ 4.9/5

Descript revolutionizes video editing by letting you edit video by editing text. If you delete a word in the transcript, it cuts the video. It also features AI voice cloning, eye contact correction, and automatic filler word removal. You can even use these automated workflows to create and sell your own digital products, like an AI art course to make money.

How to Automate Your YouTube Workflow

Setting up your AI workflow can feel overwhelming, but if you break it down into these steps, you'll be publishing faster than ever.

Step 1
💡 Ideation & Scripting

Use TubeBuddy and Jasper to generate viral video ideas and write engaging scripts with hooks and CTAs. Learn how to make money with AI tools from anywhere by scaling this process.

Step 2
🎥 Production & Editing

Use Descript to edit video by editing text, and Runway ML to generate AI B-roll and visual effects. Remove filler words and enhance audio automatically.

Step 3
🎨 SEO & Thumbnails

Optimize titles, descriptions, and tags using VidIQ, and generate click-worthy thumbnails with Canva AI or Midjourney to maximize your CTR.

Step 4
🚀 Publish & Analyze
