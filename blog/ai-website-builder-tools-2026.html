Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I really build a website without coding?

A: Absolutely! AI website builders are designed for non-technical users. If you can describe what you want, the AI will build it.

Q: How long does it take to build a website with AI?

A: Most AI builders create a complete website in 1-5 minutes. Customization adds another 15-30 minutes.

Q: Are AI-built websites SEO-friendly?

A: Yes! Top AI builders include built-in SEO optimization, meta tags, and mobile responsiveness out of the box.

Q: Can I customize the AI-generated website?

A: Yes! AI gives you a starting point. You can edit everything—text, images, colors, layout, and more.

Q: Do I own my website?

A: Yes, you own your content. However, you're typically renting the platform. If you cancel, you may lose access to the builder.

Q: Can I use my own domain?

A: Most paid plans allow custom domains. Free plans usually include the builder's branding in the URL.
