Tips for Maximum Impact

1. Tailor Every Resume

  • Never use the same resume for every job
  • Use AI to customize for each position
  • Match keywords from job description

2. Quantify Achievements

  • Use numbers and metrics
  • Show impact, not just duties
  • AI can help reframe your experience

3. Keep It Simple

  • One page for <10 years experience
  • Two pages max for senior roles
  • Clean, readable formatting

4. Proofread Everything

  • AI helps, but you must review
  • Check for typos and errors
  • Have someone else review it

📊 Success Metrics

• ATS Score: Aim for 80%+
• Keywords: Include 80%+ of job description keywords
• Readability: Keep sentences clear and concise
• Length: One page per 10 years experience
