🚀 The Complete Repurposing Blueprint

📌
Days 1-5: Twitter/X Threads (5-7 posts)

Extract 5-7 main points from your blog. Create threads with: Hook tweet (problem/question), 5-8 value tweets (one point each), CTA tweet (link to blog). Use AI to vary the hooks and angles.

📌
Days 6-12: LinkedIn Posts (5-10 posts)

Transform blog sections into professional insights. Format: Personal story hook, lesson learned, actionable takeaway, question for engagement. LinkedIn loves long-form (1000-1500 characters) with line breaks.

📌
Days 13-18: Instagram Carousels (3-5 posts)

Convert lists and frameworks into swipeable carousels. 7-10 slides: Cover slide, 5-7 value slides, CTA slide. Use AI to condense text and Canva AI for design.

📌
Days 19-24: Quote Graphics (10-15 posts)

Extract powerful quotes and statistics. Create simple text-on-background images. Post 1-2 daily for consistent presence. AI can identify the most shareable quotes.

📌
Days 25-28: Short Videos (2-3 posts)

Use OpusClip or similar to create 30-60 second videos. Read key points on camera or use AI voiceover. Add captions and post to Reels, TikTok, Shorts.

📌
Days 29-30: Facebook & Pinterest (5-10 posts)

Adapt top-performing content for Facebook groups and Pinterest boards. Create infographics and longer-form posts for these platforms.

Platform-Specific Repurposing Strategies

Each platform requires different formatting and tone. Here's how to adapt:

X/Twitter

Thread Strategy

Break blog into 8-12 tweet threads. First tweet = hook (question/statistic). Middle tweets = one idea each. Last tweet = CTA. Use emojis sparingly. Add blog link in final tweet or bio.

LinkedIn

Professional Insights

Transform blog sections into thought leadership posts. Use personal anecdotes. 1000-1500 characters optimal. Add 3-5 relevant hashtags. Post during business hours (8-10 AM, 12 PM, 5-6 PM).

Instagram

Visual Carousels

Convert lists into 7-10 slide carousels. Minimal text per slide. Strong visuals. Use Stories for behind-the-scenes. Reels for quick tips. Post 1-2x daily for best reach.

Facebook

Community Engagement

Longer posts work well (200-400 words). Ask questions to spark comments. Share in relevant groups (follow rules). Use images with every post. Best times: 1-3 PM weekdays.

Best AI Prompts for Content Repurposing

🎯 High-Converting AI Prompts

Twitter Thread Generator

"Extract 7 key points from this blog post and create an engaging Twitter thread. First tweet should be a hook that creates curiosity. Each subsequent tweet should deliver one key insight. Final tweet should include a CTA. Keep each tweet under 280 characters. Blog post: [paste content]"

2️⃣
LinkedIn Post Creator

"Transform this blog post into 5 LinkedIn posts. Each post should: start with a personal story or surprising statistic, provide actionable value, end with a question to drive engagement. Use professional tone with line breaks for readability. Blog post: [paste content]"

3️⃣
Quote Extractor

"Identify 15 powerful, shareable quotes from this blog post. Each quote should be under 100 characters and stand alone as valuable insight. Prioritize quotes with numbers, contrarian views, or actionable advice. Blog post: [paste content]"

4️⃣
Carousel Outline

"Create a 10-slide Instagram carousel outline from this blog post. Slide 1: attention-grabbing title. Slides 2-8: one key point per slide with minimal text. Slide 9: summary. Slide 10: CTA. Include visual suggestions for each slide. Blog post: [paste content]"
