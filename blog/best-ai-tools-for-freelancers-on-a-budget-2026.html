How to Build a Lean AI Workflow Without Overwhelm

Step 1: Audit Your Time Leaks

Track your work for one week. Where do you lose hours? Writing proposals? Formatting invoices? Answering the same client questions? AI isn't magic; it's a lever. Pull it where you're losing the most energy.

Step 2: Pick One Tool, Master It

Don't subscribe to everything at once. Start with ChatGPT or Canva AI. Use it daily for two weeks. Learn its quirks, refine your prompts, and measure hours saved. Consistency beats collection.

Step 3: Connect & Automate

Once you're comfortable, link tools using Zapier AI. Example: ChatGPT drafts content → Canva formats graphics → Notion stores assets → Tidio shares links with prospects. Small automations compound into massive time savings.

Step 4: Review & Trim

Every month, ask: "Is this tool still earning its keep?" If a subscription isn't saving you 5+ hours or directly generating revenue, cancel it. Lean freelancing means ruthless prioritization.
