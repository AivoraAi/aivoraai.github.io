How to Build an AI Writing Workflow That Actually Works

Step 1: Research and Outline First

Never start by asking AI to "write a blog post." That leads to generic content. Instead, use a tool like AI keyword research software or Frase to understand what your audience is searching for. Create a detailed outline with H2s and H3s based on user intent.

Step 2: Draft with Context

Feed your outline into Jasper or Surfer's AI writer. Provide context about your target audience, tone (conversational, professional, witty), and key points you want to cover. The more context you give, the less robotic the output will be.

Step 3: Add Human Value

This is the most critical step. AI can draft, but it can't replace your experience. Add personal anecdotes, unique data, screenshots, and expert quotes. Rewrite sections that feel flat or overly generic. This is what makes your content stand out in an AI-saturated world.

Step 4: Optimize and Polish

Run your draft through Surfer SEO to check keyword density, structure, and length. Then, use GrammarlyGO to fix grammar, improve clarity, and adjust tone. Finally, check for readability and add internal links to your other posts.
