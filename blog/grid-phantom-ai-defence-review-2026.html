What Is the Grid Phantom AI Defence System?

Grid Phantom is an automated trading expert advisor (EA) built for MetaTrader 4 and 5 platforms. Unlike traditional grid bots that blindly place orders at fixed intervals, Grid Phantom uses a proprietary AI algorithm to analyze market structure and volatility before deploying defensive grids.

🧠 Core Philosophy: Defense First

• Capital Preservation: The primary goal is to prevent large drawdowns during crashes.
• Smart Grid Placement: Uses AI to identify support/resistance zones rather than random pips.
• Dynamic Recovery: Automatically adjusts lot sizes and take-profit levels based on real-time market momentum.
• News Filter Integration: Can pause trading during high-impact economic events to avoid slippage.

🎯 Goal: Survive the crash, profit from the recovery

The "Phantom" aspect refers to its ability to operate invisibly in the background, making micro-adjustments to your open positions without requiring constant manual intervention. This makes it particularly appealing for traders who have day jobs or simply want to reduce screen time.

AI Logic

🤖 Adaptive Algorithm

The system learns from recent market behavior. If volatility increases, it widens grid steps to avoid premature stop-outs. If the market ranges, it tightens grids to capture small moves.

Risk Control

📉 Drawdown Limiter

A built-in hard stop feature that closes all trades if equity drops by a predefined percentage, preventing catastrophic account blowouts.

🌍 Multi-Pair Support
