How to Access Gemini AI in Google Ads

You don't need to install a separate extension. Gemini is now native to the platform. There are two primary ways to access it:

Method 1: The Conversational Experience (Campaign Creation)

When creating a new campaign, look for the option labeled "Help me create a campaign" (previously labeled as 'Conversational Experience'). This opens a chat interface where you interact with Gemini.

This is particularly useful if you're comparing Kimi AI vs Gemini AI 2026 for different marketing tasks. While Kimi excels at long-form content, Gemini shines in conversational campaign building.

Method 2: The Asset Generator

Navigate to an existing Performance Max (PMax) campaign or a Display campaign. Go to the "Assets" tab. Here you will find "Asset Creation" tools powered by Gemini that can generate text and images based on your landing page URL.

This is perfect for refreshing stale ad copy and combating creative fatigue. For more on using AI for content creation, check out our guide on Kimi AI vs Claude AI for blogging to see how different AI models handle different content types.

Step-by-Step: Building a Campaign with Gemini

Here's the exact process we use to build high-performing campaigns with Gemini AI:

Step 1: Initiate the Chat

Click the blue "+" button to create a new campaign. Select "Help me create a campaign". The first prompt you will see is something like, "Tell me about your business...".

Step 2: Write a Comprehensive Prompt

This is the most critical step. The more context you give Gemini, the better the output. Don't just say "I sell shoes." Instead, try:

"I sell sustainable vegan running shoes made from recycled plastic. My target audience is eco-conscious runners in the US and UK aged 25-40. I want to drive sales from my website with a monthly budget of $1,000."

The difference between a vague prompt and a detailed one is the difference between mediocre results and exceptional ROI. If you want to learn more about effective prompting strategies, our guide on ChatGPT for business growth covers universal prompting principles that apply to all AI models.

Step 3: Let Gemini Generate Assets

Once you hit enter, Gemini will act. It will:

  • Generate Keywords: It picks relevant keywords like "vegan running shoes", "sustainable athletic gear".
  • Write Copy: It creates 10-15 headlines and 4 descriptions.
  • Suggest Images: It generates image prompts and creates AI visuals (or suggests stock photos).

Step 4: Review and Refine

Gemini is smart, but it's not perfect. You must review the suggested assets. If you don't like a headline, type "Make the headlines more urgent" or "Focus on comfort." It will regenerate the options instantly.

This iterative process is key. For more advanced techniques on refining AI outputs, see our guide on Kimi AI for FAQ schema generation which shows how to structure prompts for specific outputs.

Using the Asset Generator for Existing Campaigns

If you already have a campaign running but are suffering from "Creative Fatigue" (people seeing the same ads too often), Gemini can help refresh your creative without starting from scratch.

  1. Go to your campaign settings.
  2. Click on Assets.
  3. Select Asset Group.
  4. Click on Generate assets.
  5. Paste your Landing Page URL.

Gemini will scan your landing page and generate new text assets and image variations that match your brand colors and messaging. We found this increased CTR by 15% in our tests by refreshing stale ad copy.

Limitations to Watch Out For

While Gemini is powerful, it has guardrails. It will not generate claims that violate Google Ads policies (like "Get rich quick" or unrealistic health claims). Sometimes, this leads to "safe" but boring ad copy.

Recommendation: Use Gemini to build the foundation (80% of the work), but manually inject your brand's unique voice (the remaining 20%) to stand out from competitors who are also using AI.

This is similar to how you'd use AI for content creation—let it do the heavy lifting, but add your human touch. For more on balancing AI efficiency with human creativity, check out our guide on Kimi AI vs Claude AI for blogging.
