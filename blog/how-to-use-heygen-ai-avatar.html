How to Create an AI Avatar Video Using HeyGen — Step-by-Step 2026

Never used HeyGen before? This complete tutorial walks you through every step — from creating your free account to exporting a polished avatar video in under 10 minutes.

Quick Answer

To create a video with HeyGen: Sign up free at heygen.com → create a new video project → choose an avatar from 200+ options → type your script → select a voice → click Generate and export. The entire process takes under 10 minutes for a typical 1-2 minute video.

Creating a professional talking-avatar video used to mean hiring an actor, booking a studio, or recording yourself a dozen times until it sounded right.

With HeyGen, the process takes under 10 minutes from blank script to finished video. Here is the exact step-by-step process, including pro tips at every stage that most tutorials skip.

Before You Start — What You Need

  • A HeyGen account (free — no credit card required)
  • Your script written out in advance
  • A paid plan if you need a watermark-free, commercially-licensed export
  • A 2–5 minute recorded video of yourself (optional, only if building a custom avatar)

⭐ Pro tip before you start: Write your script in short, conversational sentences. HeyGen's lip-sync handles natural speech patterns far more convincingly than long, formal sentences. Aim for sentences under 20 words.

Step-by-Step: Create a Video in HeyGen

1

Sign Up for a Free Account

Go to heygen.com and click "Get Started Free." Sign up with Google or email. No credit card required.

2

Create a New Video Project

Click "+ New Video" in the dashboard. Choose a blank canvas or browse pre-built templates for ads, training, or product demos.

3

Choose Your Avatar

Browse the avatar library by gender, ethnicity, age, and style. Click any avatar to preview it. To build a custom digital twin, click "Create Avatar" and upload a 2–5 minute video of yourself.

4

Write or Paste Your Script

Click on the script box and type or paste your text. Keep paragraphs short — break long scripts into multiple scenes for easier editing later.

5

Select a Voice

Choose a built-in AI voice matching your avatar, or use your own cloned voice (paid plans). Preview the voice reading your exact script before finalizing.

6

Add Background & Branding (Optional)

Upload your own background image, add your logo, or use a brand kit (Pro plan) to keep visuals consistent across videos.

7

Generate and Preview

Click "Generate" and wait for rendering — typically a few minutes depending on avatar tier and length. Preview the result before exporting.

8

Export Your Video

Choose your resolution (1080p or 4K depending on plan) and download the finished MP4 file, ready to upload anywhere.

✅ Total time: For a typical 1-2 minute script, the full process — avatar selection, script entry, voice setup, and export — takes approximately 8-12 minutes on your first try.

6 Pro Tips for Better HeyGen Videos

Tip 1 — Preview before generating. HeyGen lets you preview the voice and avatar combination before using credits to render — always check this first to avoid wasting credits.

Tip 2 — Use punctuation to control pacing. Commas create natural pauses; periods create longer breaks. This affects how natural the avatar's delivery sounds.

Tip 3 — Match avatar tone to your script. Casual UGC-style avatars suit ad content; formal stock avatars suit corporate training and explainer videos.

Tip 4 — Test custom avatars on short clips first. Before committing credits to a long video, render a 10-second test clip with your custom avatar to verify lip-sync quality.

Tip 5 — Use the translation feature for global reach. Once you have one finished video, use HeyGen's translation tool to localize it into other languages instead of recreating from scratch.

Tip 6 — Track your credit usage. Check your credit balance before starting a new project so you do not run out mid-month.
