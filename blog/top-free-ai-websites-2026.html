Common Mistakes with Free AI Tools

Avoid these pitfalls:

❌ Don't Do This

  • Ignoring limits: Not tracking usage caps
  • Quality compromise: Accepting poor outputs just because it's free
  • Security risks: Sharing sensitive data on unknown platforms
  • No backup: Relying on one free tool only
  • Not updating: Missing new free features and tools

✅ Do This Instead

  • Monitor usage: Track your free tier limits
  • Maintain quality: Edit and refine AI outputs
  • Protect data: Use reputable platforms only
  • Diversify: Have alternatives for critical tasks
