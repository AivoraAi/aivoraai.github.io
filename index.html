Honest answers to real questions about what Aivora AI is and how it works.

What is Aivora AI?+
Aivora AI is an independent AI tools review website run by Varun Lalwani. We test AI tools for writing, video, SEO, and automation, then publish honest reviews to help people decide which tools are worth using. We are not affiliated with any AI company.
Are your reviews paid or sponsored?+
No. Our reviews are never paid for or sponsored. Some links on our site are affiliate links, which means we earn a small commission if you buy through them — but this never influences what we write. If a tool is bad, we say so regardless.
Do you actually test the tools you review?+
Yes. We test each tool before writing about it. Our reviews are based on real hands-on experience, not marketing material. We note what works well and what doesn't based on actual use.
Are these tools available in India and other countries?+
Most AI tools we review are available globally. Where there are country-specific restrictions, we mention them in the review. We pay special attention to accessibility for users in India, Southeast Asia, and other emerging markets.
Is Aivora AI free to use?+
Yes, completely free. You can read all our reviews and guides without paying anything. We keep the site running through affiliate commissions when readers choose to buy tools through our links.
